The Best Snack

This is what you will need to make it

  • Different ice cream flavours
  • Nutties
  • Cake
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Chips
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

This is how you make it

  1. Put all the ice creams in to the same bowl.
  2. Then mix it with a spoon.
  3. Then put nutties on top of it.
  4. Then you put it on top of the cake.
  5. Then put chocolate syrup all over the cake and ice cream.
  6. Then put chips on the side of the cake.

That's it!! You have made the best snack.
